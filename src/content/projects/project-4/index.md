Kleaner provides a premium interface for booking and managing luxury cleaning services. The project emphasizes architectural beauty, professional photography, and a seamless client management flow.

The platform matches homeowners with vetted cleaning professionals based on service requirements, availability, and location — with 60% repeat booking rates proving the quality of the match algorithm.
